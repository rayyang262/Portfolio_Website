Battery Saver

Eliminate Battery Waste.

Introduction

Battery Saver is an eco-friendly battery disposal unit designed to reward recycling behavior and replace the generic bins people ignore. The design addresses a real and widespread problem: most battery recycling infrastructure is invisible, inconvenient, and unappealing—so it goes unused.

The project starts from the observation that sustainable behavior is rarely about values. Most people want to recycle batteries; they simply lack an accessible, satisfying way to do so. Battery Saver reframes the act of disposal as something rewarding rather than effortful, making responsible behavior the path of least resistance.

The design integrates seamlessly into everyday domestic and commercial environments, positioned at natural transition points where batteries are most likely to be changed or discarded. Its form language is approachable and tactile—designed to attract interaction rather than blend into the background like existing collection boxes.

If a sustainable product doesn’t attract use, it fails. The goal is to make the right choice feel good.

This work is part of a broader design philosophy: sustainability isn’t achieved by guilt or obligation, but by building systems that make the responsible option the most intuitive one. Battery Saver is a small intervention with measurable impact—reducing hazardous battery waste entering landfills by giving people a product they actually want to use.

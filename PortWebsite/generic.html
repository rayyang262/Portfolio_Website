About Me

Born in Switzerland, raised in Shanghai, now based in New York studying at NYU.

I work across editorial, UX, brand, and web design — not as separate disciplines, but as one interconnected practice. My background moving between cultures trained me to think across boundaries, and I design the same way: pulling from different fields to find solutions that wouldn’t exist inside a single lane.

Every project I take on is filtered through one question: does this reduce harm? I’ve spent years volunteering in waste reduction — organizing recycling drives, promoting reuse systems, pushing for less consumption. That isn’t a side interest. It’s the foundation of how I design. Sustainability isn’t an add-on to my process; it’s the constraint I start from.

I make things that are beautiful, functional, and accountable to the planet.
